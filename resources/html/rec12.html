Chilli con carne

Ingredients

  • chilli con carne mix – sachet
    •
  • red kidney beans – can
    •
  • basmati rice – 1 ½ cup
    •
  • sour cream – ½ cup
    •
  • olive oil
    •

Method

  1. Put one cup of rice and two cups of water in the rice cooker
  2. Heat up frying pan with olive oil in it
  3. Put meat in and cook till brown
  4. Add the chilli con carne mix and red kidney beans and mix till through all the mince
  5. Put rice in a bowl mince on top and add sour cream and serve
